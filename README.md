## ‚ú® Features

  * **Search Shows:** Instantly search for any shows from The Movie Database (TMDB).
  * **Add to Watchlist:** Add shows from the search results to your personal watchlist with a single click.
  * **Manage Status:** Update the status of any show on your list. Statuses include:
      * Not Watched
      * Ongoing
      * Finished
  * **Remove Shows:** Easily delete shows you no longer want on your list.
